## The One Line

Loocbooc is the universal cloud platform that gives every garment in the world a permanent digital identity — connecting manufacturers, brands, retailers, and consumers through a single living file.

---

## The Problem Worth $500 Billion

The fashion industry is the world's third largest. It runs on PDFs, spreadsheets, and emailed files. A tech pack is a PDF. A cut pattern is a DXF file on someone's desktop. A 3D sample is a garment physically shipped internationally — 4 to 6 weeks per iteration. None of these systems talk to each other.

The result: $500B+ in annual returns (30% of all online fashion purchases). Waste second only to oil. A supply chain that moves at the speed of FedEx instead of fibre optic cable.

---

## The Insight Nobody Acted On

Every garment is completely defined by two pieces of data that already exist everywhere:

1. **Geometry** — the cut pattern (digital file in every manufacturer's system)
2. **Physics** — the fabric composition (legally required on every care label globally)

Combine them → mathematically perfect 3D garment, before manufacturing begins. No scanning. No expensive equipment. The data is already there. Nobody connected it.

---

## The Solution: Universal Garment Identifier (UGI)

Every garment on Loocbooc receives a UGI — a permanent digital identity that aggregates tech pack, cut patterns, fabric physics, auto-generated 3D model, production chain, retail data, consumer interactions, and lifecycle data. One file. One ID. Every party — designer, manufacturer, retailer, consumer, recycler — connected to the same truth, in real time.

---

## The Forcing Function: EU Digital Product Passport

The EU is mandating Digital Product Passports for all textiles by 2027–2030. Every garment sold in Europe must carry a digital record of composition, origin, and lifecycle. **Loocbooc's UGI is that passport.** Every major global brand sells into Europe. They either build DPP infrastructure themselves at enormous cost — or they use Loocbooc. This is a legal mandate with a hard deadline. It is not optional. It arrives in 12–48 months.

## Go-To-Market: The Retailer Mandate Flywheel

We don't sell bottom-up to every manufacturer. We get one major retailer to mandate Loocbooc-format files from their suppliers. Suppliers adopt to keep the retailer. Manufacturers discover the efficiency gains and deploy it for all brands, not just the retailer that mandated it. More manufacturers → more brands → more retailers mandate it. The DPP is the wedge. One retailer mandate starts the flywheel.
