The Problem
The world's third-largest industry
runs on PDFs and email.
📄
Tech packs are PDFs — emailed to a factory. When something changes, someone manually re-sends the file. Factories work off the wrong version.
📁
Cut patterns live in desktop folders — AI or DXF files on someone's local machine, not connected to anything downstream.
✈️
3D samples are physical garments shipped internationally — 4–6 weeks per iteration. Each correction restarts the clock. The waste is staggering.
📦
Returns cost the industry $500B+ annually. 30% of all online fashion purchases are returned. Fit guesswork is the cause. It's a systemic problem with a structural solution.
🏭
Fashion produces more waste than any industry except oil. Overproduction, unsold inventory, and mis-sized returns. None of it is inevitable.
The Insight
Every garment is defined by
two things. Both already exist.
📐
Geometry
The cut pattern. Already exists as a digital file in every manufacturer's system. It defines every dimension, every seam, every curve of the garment.
+
🧵
Physics
The fabric composition. Legally required on every care label globally. Composition maps deterministically to drape, weight, stretch, and recovery.
→ Mathematically perfect 3D garment. Before it's manufactured.
No scanning. No expensive equipment. No sample shipping. The data already exists in every factory. We connect it.
